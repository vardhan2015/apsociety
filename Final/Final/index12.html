About Us

Vardhan Multi-State Co-operative Society Ltd. was established in 2015 under the Multi State Co-operative Societies Act 2002.

Co-operative is an organization wherein persons voluntarily associated together as human collective resource on the basis of equality for the promotion of the economic interest of themselves. Co-operation is a self-help and mutual help to achieve the common goal.
