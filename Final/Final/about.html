Registration of Vardhan Mutually Aided Co-Operative Thrift Society as Multi-State Co-Operative Society under Section 103(1) of the MSCS Act, 2002

In reference your letters dated 23/2/2015 and 06/04/2015 on the above mentioned subject the Society has been granted Multi State vide certificate of registration dated , however, it is observed that the proposed text of bye-laws submitted by the Society is deficient. The bye-laws have not been framed as per the provisions of MSCS Act, 2002 & Rules made thereunder.

The Society is requested to refer to the provisions of MSCS Act, 2002 & Rules made trhereunder and the society may also refer to the model bye-laws available on the website of the Ministry of Agriculture i.e., www.mscs.dsc.gov.in for framing the bye-laws. The Society shall also ensure that:
1) The word 'LTD'. is suffixed to the namne of the society.

3) Area of operation of the society shall be as mentioned in the certificate.
